✦ About Kerala

Kerala — An Overview

Kerala, tucked into India's southwestern tip, is a land of extraordinary natural beauty and cultural richness. Fringed by 600 km of coastline and laced with a network of serene backwaters, this emerald state is famous for its houseboat cruises, Kathakali dance, and ancient Ayurvedic healing traditions.

The Western Ghats, one of the world's biodiversity hotspots, rise dramatically from the coastal plains, carpeted with tea, coffee, and spice plantations. From the misty peaks of Munnar to the vibrant shores of Kovalam, Kerala is a state that engages all the senses — the scent of cardamom, the sight of Chinese fishing nets at dusk, the sound of classical music echoing through ancient temples.

Top Experiences in Kerala

🛥️
Alleppey Houseboat
Float through Kerala's legendary backwaters on a traditional kettuvallam houseboat, complete with a personal chef and sundeck.
☕
Munnar Tea Gardens
Trek through the verdant tea estates of Munnar at dawn, witness tea pluckers at work, and visit the tea museum.
🧘
Ayurvedic Retreat
Experience authentic Ayurvedic treatments — from Abhyanga oil massages to Panchakarma — at certified wellness resorts.
🐘
Periyar Wildlife
Spot wild elephants, gaur, and deer on a boat safari through Periyar Lake in the heart of a tiger reserve.
💃
Kathakali Performance
Watch the mesmerising Kathakali dance-drama — a centuries-old classical art form with elaborate makeup and storytelling.
🏖️
Kovalam Beaches
Relax on the crescent-shaped Lighthouse Beach at Kovalam, one of India's most picturesque coastlines.
