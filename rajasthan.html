✦ About Rajasthan

Rajasthan — An Overview

Rajasthan, the Land of Kings, is India's largest state and most visited destination. Its landscape is a grand canvas of sand dunes, ancient fortresses, ornate havelis, and shimmering lakes. Every city tells a story — Jaipur's pink streets, Udaipur's romantic lake palaces, Jodhpur's blue lanes, and Jaisalmer's golden sandstone fortress rising from the Thar Desert.

From the opulent heritage hotels that were once maharaja palaces to the humbling spirituality of its temples, Rajasthan offers a journey through centuries of royal history, warrior culture, and living art forms. Its cuisine — dal baati churma, laal maas, and gatte ki sabzi — is as bold and colourful as its people.

✦ Highlights

Top Experiences in Rajasthan

🏯
Amber Fort & City Palace
Explore the stunning hilltop Amber Fort and the opulent City Palace complex of Jaipur, showcasing Rajput architecture at its finest.
🐪
Thar Desert Safari
Experience a camel safari across the golden Thar Desert, watching the sunset paint the dunes in shades of amber and crimson.
🏝️
Lake Pichola, Udaipur
Cruise across the tranquil Lake Pichola in Udaipur to the iconic Lake Palace — a floating marble marvel.
🦚
Ranthambore Wildlife
Track the majestic Bengal tiger in Ranthambore National Park, one of India's premier wildlife sanctuaries.
🎨
Local Arts & Crafts
Discover block printing, blue pottery, miniature paintings, and gem-cutting — crafts passed down through generations.
🎪
Pushkar Camel Fair
If visiting in November, experience the world-famous Pushkar Camel Fair — a spectacle of colour, culture and commerce.
