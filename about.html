✦ Our Story

Born from a Love for India

Aaradhya Tours was born in 2012 from a simple belief: that India is the world's most extraordinary travel destination, and every visitor deserves to experience its soul, not just its surface.

Founded in Jaipur by Rajan Mehta — himself a passionate traveller who had explored every corner of the subcontinent — we started with a small team, big dreams, and an unshakeable commitment to authentic experiences.

Today, we are a pan-India operator trusted by over 12,000 travellers from across the globe. Our expert team of 40+ travel specialists, local guides, and destination experts work tirelessly to ensure each journey is as unique as the person taking it.
